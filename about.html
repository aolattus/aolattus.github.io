About Me

I am a PhD candidate in economics at Brown University. My research focuses on macroeconomics, finance, behavioral finance, and food policy.

Before coming to Brown, I graduated from Arizona State University with a bachelor's degree in economics (with honors) and minors in mathematics and French.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading, cooking, traveling, and hanging out with my cat. She is clearly much cooler than I am.
